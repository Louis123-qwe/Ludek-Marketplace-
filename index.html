How Ludek Works

Three simple steps to buy or sell anything on campus. No stress, no complications.

01

Create Account

Sign up with your email for free. Choose your role — buyer, seller, tutor, or housing agent.

02

Post a Listing

Snap a photo, set your price, choose a category and post your listing in under 2 minutes.

03

Connect via WhatsApp

Buyers tap "Chat Seller" to open a WhatsApp conversation with prefilled product details instantly.

04

Deal & Done

Meet on campus, exchange, and done. Clean, simple, and fully within your student community.
